# Album 31 – Psalm 119 Tamil Worship Songs (Mem to Qoph)

Mem (97-104): God’s Word sweeter than honey, source of wisdom & meditation.
Nun (105-112): Word is lamp to feet, eternal heritage & joy.
Samekh (113-120): Hate double-minded, uphold by Word.
Ayin (121-128): Long for salvation, love commandments.
Pe (129-136): Wonderful testimonies, light & understanding.
Tsadhe (137-144): Righteous & faithful Word, zeal for it.
Qoph (145-152): Wholehearted cry, early morning prayer, nearness of God.

Remnant Tamil Worship – Freely given, All glory to Jesus Christ. ஆமென் | அல்லேலூயா


Tamil Christian Worship Album based on Psalm 119 (Mem to Pe sections).

Songs:
1. தேனிலும் மதுரமானது | Sweeter Than Honey (119:97-104)
2. என் கால்களுக்கு தீபம் | Lamp to My Feet (119:105-112)
3. என் மறைவிடம் என் கேடகம் | You Are My Hiding Place (119:113-120)
4. பொன்னிலும் பசும்பொன்னிலும் | More Than Gold (119:121-128)
5. கர்த்தருடைய வசனத்தின் வெளிச்சம் வந்ததே | The Light of Your Word (119:129-136)

All songs are dedicated to Jesus Christ, King of Kings and Lord of Lords.
